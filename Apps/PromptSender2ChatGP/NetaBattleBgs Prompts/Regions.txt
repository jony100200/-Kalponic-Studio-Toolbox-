Good. This is the right move.
If themes are strong, your whole pack feels original instead of generic fantasy spam.

Below is a **clean, production-focused theme list** based on **underused regions + cultural + biome identity**. These are **game-ready directions**, not vague ideas.

---

# 🌍 PRIMARY UNUSED REGION THEMES

## 1 — Sub-Saharan Africa (Savanna / Tribal / Ancient)

* baobab trees, dry grasslands
* tribal stone circles, masks, carvings
* red earth, dust wind
* sunset heavy lighting

👉 Strong identity, rarely done properly

---

## 2 — Indian Subcontinent (Mythic + Historical Blend)

* temple ruins, carved pillars
* stepwells, courtyards
* monsoon wet ground
* sacred trees, lamps

👉 Huge variety: jungle + desert + sacred + royal

---

## 3 — Mongolian Steppe / Central Asia

* endless grass plains
* stone totems, wind-carved rocks
* yurts (distant), banners
* wide open sky

👉 Very clean battlefield readability

---

## 4 — Arabian / Persian Desert Fantasy

* sandstone cities, arches
* oasis, carpets, lanterns
* dunes + ruins combo
* golden warm lighting

👉 Strong commercial appeal

---

## 5 — Southeast Asia (Jungle Temple)

* overgrown ruins
* thick jungle vegetation
* stone statues
* humid fog

👉 Good for magical + natural fusion

---

## 6 — Tibetan / Himalayan Highlands

* snowy cliffs
* prayer flags
* stone monasteries
* thin air atmosphere

👉 Unique vertical + spiritual vibe

---

## 7 — Mesoamerican (Aztec / Mayan Style)

* pyramid ruins
* jungle stone carvings
* sacrificial platforms
* gold + jade accents

👉 Very strong visual identity

---

## 8 — Ancient Egypt (But DONE DIFFERENT)

* buried ruins
* broken obelisks
* underground tomb halls
* sand-covered structures

👉 Avoid cliché pyramids → go deeper

---

## 9 — Slavic / Eastern European Dark Forest

* crooked trees
* wooden shrines
* fog + swamp
* old folklore vibe

👉 Great for darker tone packs

---

## 10 — Nordic / Viking Lands

* rocky coasts
* frozen plains
* wooden ruins
* aurora sky

👉 Popular but still expandable

---

# 🔥 BEST COMBINATION STRATEGY (IMPORTANT)

Don’t make themes isolated. Combine:

### Example combos:

* African + Spirit Realm
* Indian + Sacred Grounds
* Mongolian + Storm Plains
* Arabian + Corrupted Desert
* Jungle Temple + Ancient Battlefield

👉 This is where originality comes from.
