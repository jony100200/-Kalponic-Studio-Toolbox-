# 🔥 MASTER MATERIAL LIST (GAME-READY CATEGORIES)

Use this as your production checklist.

---

# 🌿 1. ORGANIC / NATURAL

**Core sellers for games**

* Grass (short, tall, wild, glowing)
* Dirt (dry, wet, muddy, cracked)
* Sand (fine, dune, rocky desert)
* Snow (soft, packed, icy mix)
* Leaves (green, autumn, jungle dense)
* Moss (light, heavy, creeping)
* Bark (rough, smooth, stylized)
* Roots (exposed, tangled)
* Stone (flat, rough, carved)
* Rock (large chunks, gravel mix)
* Clay / Earth (soft, compact)
* Ash / Dust (volcanic, powdery)

---

# 🪵 2. WOOD

**Very important for fantasy + medieval**

* Wooden planks (clean, worn)
* Old wood (cracked, aged)
* Painted wood (peeling paint)
* Carved wood (ornamental)
* Bamboo (clean, split, woven)
* Logs (cut, natural)
* Parquet floor (stylized)

---

# 🪨 3. STONE / CONSTRUCTION

**High demand category**

* Cobblestone (tight, loose)
* Brick (clean, worn, broken)
* Marble (polished, cracked)
* Granite (rough, heavy)
* Slate (layered stone)
* Temple stone (carved patterns)
* Ruins stone (broken slabs)
* Pavement (urban, fantasy)

---

# 🏺 4. CERAMIC / TILE / MOSAIC

**Underrated but very valuable**

* Ceramic tiles (clean, worn)
* Mosaic tiles (Islamic / Persian / African patterns)
* Glazed tiles (shiny, reflective)
* Terracotta (warm clay tiles)
* Decorative floor tiles (ornate)
* Cracked tiles (aged)
* Painted tiles (stylized patterns)

---

# ⚙️ 5. METAL

**For sci-fi + industrial + fantasy armor style**

* Steel plates (clean, worn)
* Iron (rusty, aged)
* Bronze (ancient, oxidized)
* Gold (ornate, polished)
* Copper (green patina)
* Metal panels (sci-fi)
* Riveted metal (industrial)
* Grates / vents
* Scratched metal
* Brushed metal

---

# 🧪 6. SCI-FI / SYNTHETIC

**High value for modern games**

* Plastic (matte, glossy)

* Neon panels
* Holographic surfaces

# 🔥 SIMPLE RULE FOR GENERATION

Every texture must follow:

* ✅ Large readable shapes (no noise)
* ✅ Soft gradients (Ghibli feel)
* ✅ Tileable
* ✅ Top-down
* ✅ Minimal harsh contrast
